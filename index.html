My Cause

We should support anime in Japan as a society because of the main challenges in the anime industry, particularly for animators, and how it affects their livelihood and relationships. For example, it is a time-consuming job with low pay compared to other equally important roles. This may eliminate the possibility of it being a future career for aspiring animators or anime fans.

My major message to you is that you have the power to alter the future of anime animators, whether they are aspiring, present, or former.
